Cancellation Policy

Subscription Cancellations (Chatchapiti Kit)

Subscriptions for Chatchapiti Kit can be canceled at any time through the customer portal provided by Lemon Squeezy. Your access to the service will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How to Cancel Your Subscription:
  1. Access your Lemon Squeezy customer portal using the link provided in your purchase confirmation email
  2. Log in with your purchase email address
  3. Navigate to your active subscriptions
  4. Click "Cancel Subscription" for Chatchapiti Kit
  5. Confirm the cancellation
Important Cancellation Details:
  • No cancellation fees or penalties
  • Access continues until the end of your billing period
  • No pro-rated refunds for partial billing periods
  • You can resubscribe at any time
  • Your data and settings are preserved for 30 days after cancellation

Service Project Cancellations

For consulting and development services, cancellation terms are outlined in individual service agreements. Generally, clients can cancel projects with appropriate notice, though completed work phases remain billable.

Standard Service Cancellation Terms:
  • 7-day written notice required for ongoing projects
  • Completed work phases are non-refundable

  • Final deliverables provided upon payment of outstanding amounts
