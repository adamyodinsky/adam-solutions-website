Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). For urgent technical support issues, we aim to respond within 4-8 hours.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations for custom development and DevOps consulting projects. This helps us understand your needs and provide accurate project estimates.

What information should I include in my project inquiry?

Please include your project goals, technical requirements, timeline, budget range, and any specific technologies or constraints. The more details you provide, the better we can assist you.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally. Our digital products are available worldwide, and we provide remote consulting and development services across different time zones.

How do I cancel my Chatchapiti Kit subscription?

Subscriptions can be canceled anytime through the customer portal provided by Lemon Squeezy. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and various international payment methods through our secure payment processor, Lemon Squeezy.
